What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, the nerve that transmits images from the eye to the brain. This damage can lead to vision loss and, if left untreated, blindness.

Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Our thorough eye exams are designed to detect glaucoma and other eye conditions before they lead to severe vision loss.

Visual Field Testing

We use advanced visual field testing to evaluate your peripheral vision and check for subtle early signs of glaucoma.

Optic Nerve & Retinal Evaluation

Our specialized imaging and examination techniques help us assess the health of your optic nerve and retina, crucial areas affected by glaucoma.
